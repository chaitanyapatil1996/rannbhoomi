Brand Philosophy

THE ARENA
WITHIN

RANNBHOOMI represents more than a battlefield.

It symbolizes the arena that exists within every individual — a place where strength, discipline, and resilience are tested.

Every challenge, every competition, and every moment of endurance begins with an internal battle. RANNBHOOMI celebrates those who step into that arena and push beyond their limits.

The identity is built around the philosophy that true warriors are forged not just through conflict, but through control, resilience, and self-mastery.

Workout Standards

RULES

01
Burpee Standards
Chest must clearly touch the ground at the bottom. Both feet must leave the ground during the jump. Full body extension at the top required.
1 Burpee = 10 pts
02
Push-Up Standards
Chest must touch the floor completely. Elbows must be fully locked at the top position. No resting at the bottom. Strict form required throughout the set.
03
Sprint Standards
Athlete must cross or touch the line on both ends. Both feet must cross the line for the rep to count.
1 way = 20 metres = 20 pts
04
Eco Bike Standards
Athlete may dismount only after referee confirmation. Machine must reach target distance or calorie before rider dismounts.
1 metre = 1 pt
05
Overhead KB Walk
Athlete must cross the line with both feet completely outside at each turn. Full elbow lockout overhead throughout the walk.
20 metres = 20 pts
06
General Rules
All movements subject to referee judgment. No-rep decisions are final. Unsportsmanlike behaviour will result in penalties or disqualification.

Questions

FAQ

RANNBHOOMI 2026 is open to all adults who are physically fit and ready to challenge themselves. Both male and female athletes compete in separate categories. Athletes must be capable of performing the programmed movements safely and with proper form.
Three rounds: Round 1 (Qualifiers) — AMRAP across 7 stations, 2 min per station, 12-min time cap, top 30 each gender advance. Round 2 (Semi-Finals) — AMRAP in 20 minutes: Rowing + Devil's Press + KB Walk + Box Jumps. Round 3 (Finals) — Full circuit for minimum time with athlete-chosen weights, top 10 each gender compete.
Scoring varies by movement: Burpees & Push-ups = 10 pts/rep. Sprint = 20 pts per 20m. Bike = 1 pt/metre. Rowing = 1 pt/calorie. KB Walk = 20 pts per 20m. Box Jumps = 10 pts/rep. Finals scoring is based on total weight selected by the athlete as approved by the referee.
Yes. All movements are subject to referee judgment. No-rep decisions by the referee are final and binding. Athletes are encouraged to review the full movement standards document before competing. Unsportsmanlike behaviour will result in penalties or disqualification.
The Finals workout will be revealed on competition day. The format involves Thrusters, Sled Push, KB Forward Carry Lunges, Box Step-Ups, Rower, and a Sprint Finish. Athletes select their own weights — strategic strength and endurance are both tested.
All competition equipment is provided at the venue. Athletes should bring appropriate athletic footwear, workout attire, personal chalk if preferred, a water bottle, and their competitive spirit. All machines, barbells, dumbbells, kettlebells, sleds, sandbags, and boxes are supplied.

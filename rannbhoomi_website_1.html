ABOUT RANNBHOOMI

Built to answer one question: what does real fitness look like under pressure?

Rannbhoomi was born from the belief that true athletic capability cannot be memorised or rehearsed in a controlled setting. It surfaces when the plan falls apart — when unpredictability enters the equation. This is fitness with purpose: fitness that prepares you for sport, work, family, life, and the unexpected.

Where pressure creates performance. Where ordinary individuals discover extraordinary resilience. A battlefield for those willing to test themselves beyond comfort.

Shubham Toonwal
Performance Coach · Certified Trainer · National-Level Athlete

As a national-level athlete and performance coach, Shubham has trained individuals across fitness levels and seen firsthand how structured training fails when unpredictability enters the equation. His vision for Rannbhoomi: reward real athletic capability — not memorised performance.

Veenita Johnson
Hybrid Athlete · Hyrox Performance Coach · Community Builder

Through years of coaching and competing, Veenita recognised the need for a platform that combines performance, inclusivity, and real-world functionality. Her contribution to Rannbhoomi: experiences that challenge athletes holistically — physically, mentally, and emotionally.

Neha Barman
HR & Branding Leader · Fitness Entrepreneur

Having balanced work, family, fitness, and growth firsthand, Neha envisioned Rannbhoomi as more than a competition — a platform that empowers everyday individuals to discover the athlete within themselves.

Workout Standards

RULES

01
Burpee Standards
Chest must clearly touch the ground at the bottom. Both feet must leave the ground during the jump. Full body extension at the top required.
1 Burpee = 10 pts
02
Hands Release Push Up Standards
Chest must fully contact the floor on every rep. Hands must visibly leave the floor at the bottom before the next rep. Full elbow lockout required at the top. No resting at the bottom.
1 Rep = 5 pts
03
Sprint Standards
Athlete must cross or touch the line on both ends. Both feet must cross the line for the rep to count.
1 way = 20 metres = 20 pts
04
Erg Bike Standards
Athlete may dismount only after referee confirmation. Machine must reach target distance or calorie before rider dismounts.
1 metre = 1 pt
05
Overhead KB Walk
Athlete must cross the line with both feet completely outside at each turn. Full elbow lockout overhead throughout the walk.
20 metres = 20 pts
06
Deadlift Standards
Full hip and knee extension at lockout on every rep. Dumbbells must touch the floor between reps. Back must remain neutral throughout — no rounding. Hips may not rise before the weight moves.
1 Rep = 10 pts
07
DB Front Squat Standards
Dumbbells must remain at shoulder height throughout the movement. Hip crease must pass below the top of the knee at the bottom. Full hip and knee extension required at the top.
1 Rep = 5 pts
08
Inch Worm Standards
Start standing with feet hip-width. Walk hands forward to a fully locked-out plank position, then walk feet up to meet the hands. Full plank lockout and feet at hands both required for the rep to count.
1 Rep = 10 pts
09
General Rules
All movements subject to referee judgment. No-rep decisions are final. Unsportsmanlike behaviour will result in penalties or disqualification.

Questions

FAQ

RANNBHOOMI 2026 is open to all adults who are physically fit and ready to challenge themselves. Both male and female athletes compete in separate categories. Athletes must be capable of performing the programmed movements safely and with proper form.
Three battles: Battle 1 (Qualifiers) — AMRAP across 7 stations, 2 min per station, 12-min time cap, top 30 each gender advance. Battle 2 (Semi-Finals) — AMRAP in 20 minutes: Rowing + Devil's Press + KB Walk + Box Jumps. Battle 3 (Finals) — workout revealed after Battle 2 on competition day, top 10 each gender compete.
Scoring varies by movement. Battle 1: Burpees = 10 pts/rep · Hands Release Push Ups = 5 pts/rep · Deadlift = 10 pts/rep · DB Front Squats = 5 pts/rep · Inch Worms = 10 pts/rep · Sprint with Weights = 20 pts/lap · Erg Bike = 1 pt/metre. Battle 2: Rowing = 1 pt/metre · Devil's Press = 10 pts/rep · Overhead KB Walk = 20 pts per 20m · Burpee Box Jump = 10 pts/rep. Battle 3 scoring will be revealed on competition day.
Yes. All movements are subject to referee judgment. No-rep decisions by the referee are final and binding. Athletes are encouraged to review the full movement standards document before competing. Unsportsmanlike behaviour will result in penalties or disqualification.
The Battle 3 workout will be revealed after Battle 2 on competition day. Only the top 10 Male & 10 Female athletes who advance from Battle 2 will find out. Strategic strength, endurance, and the ability to adapt to the unknown are all tested.
All competition equipment is provided at the venue. Athletes should bring appropriate athletic footwear, workout attire, personal chalk if preferred, a water bottle, and their competitive spirit. All machines, barbells, dumbbells, kettlebells, sleds, sandbags, and boxes are supplied.
Registration pricing will be announced soon. Early bird pricing will be available for a limited period. Follow @rannbhoomi on Instagram or check back on this page for updates. Refund and cancellation terms will be published alongside pricing.
Battle 1 runs 4 athletes at a time across 7 stations (2 minutes per station, 30 seconds rest between). Each wave takes approximately 20 minutes. The full Battle 1 qualifier is scheduled to run over approximately 2 hours 40 minutes. Battle 2 and Battle 3 timing will be confirmed closer to the event. Plan to arrive at least 30 minutes before your wave.
